Shoe Salon

Logo and Ads

Answering the call of luxury shoe lovers, The Shoe Salon carry brands such as Manolo Blahnik, Jil Sander, Marc Jacobs, Kenzo, and Bruno Magli.

The identity was designed to give a glimpse of what the brand is about and marketed with image-driven campaigns to express individuality, product personalities, and stories through art and style.
