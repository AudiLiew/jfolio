Fabulous Aesthetics

Social Media Content and Advertising

Fabulous Aesthetics is an award-winning beauty and wellness brand that is committed to empowering men and women with the confidence that beauty provides.

As traditional salons had to digitally transform to stay relevant, I relied on visual metaphors to be a thumb-stopper on social for the brand. Images used for advertising campaign were successful in getting the message across at the same time meeting Facebook's strict advertising policies on beauty campaigns.
